The Challenges Camp Directors Face Today

Keeping staff safe and connected across sprawling camp properties is harder than it should be. We've talked to camp directors who describe the same frustrations:

Staff is scattered across camp with no reliable way to reach everyone instantly

Weather monitoring is manual and reliant on someone checking

No way to confirm critical messages were received

Communication is fragmented across multiple services

Emergency documentation depends on staff manually recording incidents in real time

We're Building a Better Way

Chirp is a mobile app designed specifically to leverage smartphone hardware for camp staff communication and weather safety — bringing automated alerts with attached response protocols, instant messaging, and accountability together in one platform that's built for how camps actually operate.

Parents Hear It From You First

When something happens at camp, the clock starts immediately. Parents talk to each other. News travels. Social media moves fast. If parents learn about an incident from another source before hearing from you, trust erodes instantly — even if you handled everything perfectly.

Chirp ensures parents hear it from you first — not from another parent, not from social media, not from the news.

  • Parent notifications reach every family in seconds
  • Multi-channel delivery: push notification, SMS, and email
  • Delivery confirmation shows who received the message and when
  • Chirp enables camps to create their own alert templates so you aren't writing from scratch during a crisis
  • Message confirmation tracking that parents were informed

Every Camper, Accounted For

During emergencies, the location of every staff member is critical. Paper-based head counts fail under pressure. In large camps where staff is spread across multiple activities, accounting for everyone is difficult at best.

When you trigger a missing camper alert, Chirp doesn't just notify your team — it includes the camper's photo and relevant details so everyone knows exactly who they're looking for.

  • Complete camper profiles with photos, important medical information, and emergency contact info
  • Groups inside of Chirp are called Flocks and Flock assignments link campers to their counselors and cabins
  • Digital muster check-ins at designated safety zones
  • Real-time alert response acknowledgement during emergencies
