How Chirp Compares

Most camp platforms charge per total camper enrolled — meaning every session multiplies your cost. Chirp charges on the max campers you expect on-site at once. Run 5 sessions with 200 campers each? You pay for 200, not 1,000.

Pricing Questions

How do you determine my camp's capacity for pricing?

When you sign up, you tell us the maximum number of campers you expect to have on-site at any one time during your active period. That single number sets your monthly rate for the entire period. It's a simple self-declaration — and since Chirp tracks camper profiles and muster check-ins, the platform naturally reflects your actual usage. No license paperwork, no complicated formulas.

What if my camp runs multiple sessions?

You pay the same monthly rate regardless of how many sessions you run. A camp that declares 250 max campers on-site pays $250/month whether you run one session or five. We price on capacity, not throughput.

How does sleep mode work?

Use Chirp for at least 4 months per billing year, and you qualify for sleep mode at 1/3 your active rate (minimum $50/month) for the remaining months. A third of the price for using us a third of the year. Sleep mode is one contiguous period — activate it when your season ends, and everything is exactly where you left it when you wake up.

Are there any setup fees or contracts?

No setup fees. No long-term contracts. You can set up Chirp directly from the app in a day or two. If you want us to handle the setup for you, we offer that as a free onboarding service.

What if my camp has fewer than 100 campers?

The minimum active rate is $100/month, which covers camps with up to 100 campers. If your expected max is under 100, you still get the full platform at $100/month. Sleep mode minimum is $50/month.

Do you charge per staff member?

No. Unlimited staff users are included at every level. Your directors, counselors, medical staff, and administrators all get full access at no additional cost.
