About Chirp

Our Story

Chirp started with a phone call from a good friend and executive director of a Texas camp, who needed help navigating the new Heaven 27 regulations. What began as a simple weather detection and alert system quickly grew as conversations with camp directors revealed bigger needs around communication, documentation, and coordination.

Today, Chirp is a full camp safety and critical communications platform — covering weather alerts, emergency broadcasts, staff messaging, and audit logs — all in one place, built to adapt to the way each camp actually operates.

What Drives Us

For too long, legacy companies have dominated the camp industry — providing outdated, underperforming software at modern-day prices. We built Chirp with direct input from camp directors to deliver what camps actually need, while ensuring that the value of our product well exceeds the cost.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't like my counselors using their personal phones?

Chirp is designed to run on any phone, so camps have the option to purchase a set of dedicated devices that are distributed to staff at the beginning of each session. This way, your team gets full access to Chirp's safety and communication features without the risk of personal phone use during camp hours.

How does Chirp function in offline environments?

Chirp uses a hybrid offline strategy combining mesh networking, strategic repeater placement, and satellite fallback. Staff carry small, affordable mesh devices that pair with their phones via Bluetooth — messages hop through the network and sync to the cloud when connectivity returns. Emergency alerts are always prioritized for delivery, even in the most remote areas.

How does Chirp's pricing scale?

Pricing is based on your camp's unique camper count per year, with flat tiers that scale as you grow. There are no per-user fees, no hidden costs, and no features locked behind higher plans — every camp gets the full platform. Our pricing is published transparently, no sales calls required.

What if my camp doesn't run year round?

Chirp offers a sleep mode for seasonal camps. You can pause your account once per year during your off-season and pay a reduced hosting fee to keep your camp's data stored and ready. When your next session begins, everything is right where you left it.

What's the setup process for integrating Chirp into my camp?

Chirp's setup process is built directly into the app and designed to be fast and painless — no outside tools or technical setup required (if you can use a smartphone, you can set up Chirp). A camp can be up and running in as little as a day or two. If you'd prefer someone to handle it for you, we offer a setup service where a Chirp team member configures everything on your behalf.
